Planner · weekly robot schedule

Every robot-task in time order. Drag-reorder + constraint warnings are coming in the next pass — for now you can see the computed schedule and let the optimizer suggest a better one.

Each row is a day. Solid chips are robot-served steps (Dips 1-7, Seal). Dashed chips with an OP badge are operator-run steps (Hang, Sand cleaning, Unhang) — done manually on the floor, in parallel with the robot. Both kinds are draggable: click a chip to pick it (navy halo), then click another chip to swap positions or click a day's ＋ zone to append at the end of that day. Moves that would break a batch's pipeline order (e.g. Dip 2 before Dip 1) are rejected. The amber "Past window" row holds tasks the plant can't reach in 7 days.
